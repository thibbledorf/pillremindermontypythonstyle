# Privacy Policy

## Pill Reminder - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 17, 2026

### Overview

Pill Reminder ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### Information You Provide Directly
- **Reminder Settings:** Medication times, condition type (e.g., "Parkinsons"), voice preferences (rate, volume)
- **Optional Settings:** API keys (if you choose to enable AI phrase generation)

#### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal health data beyond what you voluntarily enter
- Location information (unless you explicitly enable weather)
- Contact information
- Demographic information
- Browsing history
- Crash reports or analytics
- Advertising identifiers

### 2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:
- Display and schedule your medication reminders
- Generate voice alerts with your preferences
- Persist your settings between app launches
- (Optional) Fetch weather and news when you enable those features
- (Optional) Generate new phrases if you provide an Anthropic API key

### 3. Data Storage and Security

**Local Storage:**
- All reminder data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
- Your data does not leave your device unless you explicitly request a feature that requires it

**Optional Cloud Features:**
- If you provide an Anthropic API key for AI phrase generation, that key is stored securely in your device's Keychain
- API calls to Anthropic are made directly from your device over HTTPS
- We do not store, log, or have access to your API key

**Weather and News:**
- Weather data comes from wttr.in (free API, no authentication required)
- News comes from public RSS feeds (Fox News)
- These requests are made from your device; we do not log them

### 4. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** share your data with any third parties. Your data stays on your device.

**Exceptions:**
- If you enable AI phrase generation, Anthropic receives your phrase generation requests (you control this with your own API key)
- If you enable weather, wttr.in receives your location for weather lookup
- If you enable news, Fox News RSS feeds are accessed

You have full control over these optional features and can disable them at any time.

### 5. Data Retention

- Reminder data is retained until you delete it
- Deleted reminders are removed from your device
- There is no cloud backup or recovery service

### 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (it's all on your device)
- Delete your data (use "Clear All Reminders" in Settings)
- Disable optional features (weather, news, AI generation) at any time
- Request no collection of optional data (don't enable those features)

### 7. Children's Privacy

Pill Reminder is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete such information immediately.

### 8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect, sell, or share personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the service, most CCPA requirements are not applicable. However:

- You have the right to know what data we collect (see Section 1)
- You have the right to delete your data (see Section 6)
- You have the right to opt-out of sales (we do not sell data)

### 9. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- Right to access your data
- Right to rectify (correct) your data
- Right to erasure (deletion)
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you can access, modify, or delete it directly through the app.

### 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
